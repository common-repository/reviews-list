The most popular video-games and high-tech products reviews in a one simple list grouped by category.

== Description ==

Display a list of the most popular products reviews (video-games or high-tech) grouped by category that you can choose.

You can choose the language for the widget :
. English
. Français
and of course the category of reviewed products (games, smartphones, laptop....)

(in french :) Affiche une liste des produits testés les plus populaires (jeux videos ou high-tech), groupés par catégorie.
Vous pouvez choisir la langue (anglais ou français), et bien sûr la catégorie des produits testés (jeux, téléphones portables, etc...)

== Installation ==

1. Upload the plugin files to the `/wp-content/plugins/reviewsList` directory, or install the plugin through the WordPress plugins screen directly.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' screen in WordPress
3. Use the Appearance->Widget->reviewsList screen to place the widget, choose the language and the category of products to display


== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= What is CommentChoisir.fr ? =

CommentChoisir.fr (www.CommentChoisir.fr/en/ in english) regroup all the reviews find in internet for a video-game or a high-tech product in an one single page : 
The rating, reviews, the "pros & cons" in one page to help you choose the game or the product you want !

= Can i change language ? =

Yes, everytime you want, go to 'Appearance->Widget->reviewsList' to change the language :
. English
. Français
